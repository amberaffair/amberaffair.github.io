Delivery

Standard delivery items will be sent by the Royal Mail “Signed for” delivery service. Item(s) will require a signature from you upon arrival. This service gives you peace of mind that your item(s) will arrive safely and directly into your possession. This delivery option is £2.50 regardless of how many items are ordered. Please note that all orders over £100 are automatically sent free via Special Delivery. Our aim is to deliver all UK orders within 3-5 working days of your payment being received.
Customers from Europe or overseas deliveries will be charged £8.50 postage. Goods will be sent airmail recorded delivery. Please allow up to 5-10 days for delivery.
